Definitions

These labels are included near the model so readers do not confuse model mechanics with market share, disease burden, or pricing categories.

Source pool

Eyes grouped by current treatment source before the model applies durable-option consideration, access, adoption, and competition.

Eligible and accessible

Source eyes that remain clinically plausible and reachable after clinical suitability, payer access, site readiness, and annual access timing are applied.

Durable option

A longer-duration wet AMD treatment choice. Ixo-vec receives a modeled allocation within this broader durable-treatment class.

Treated eyes

Modeled Ixo-vec treatment courses in a year. This is not market share and is not a price tier by disease burden.
